How to Find the Serial Number of Your ASUS Device ?

Your ASUS serial number is a unique identifier used for warranty, support, and product tracking. Here's how to quickly locate it across different types of devices.

What is a Serial Number?

A serial number (S/N) is a unique code assigned to each device to distinguish it from others. It's often required for:

  • Warranty or repair requests
  • Live chat and customer support
  • Firmware and driver updates
  • Product registration
